Why choose Dr. Mohamad Yazbeck?

Dr. Mohamad Yazbeck is a highly specialized neurosurgeon combining advanced surgical techniques with extensive clinical experience. His commitment to patient care, innovation, and precision makes him a trusted expert in complex neurological procedures.

Clinical Expertise and Advanced Techniques

  • Awake craniotomy to preserve speech and motor areas during tumor resection.
  • Use of MRI Tractography to map neural pathways before and during surgery.
  • Complex skull base surgeries (transnasal or transcranial) in collaboration with top French centers.
  • Minimally invasive spinal surgery and disc replacement.
  • Tumor resection from areas responsible for facial movement and speech.
