Why choose Dr. Mohammad Yezbeck?

Dr. Mohammad Yezbeck is a highly specialized neurosurgeon combining advanced surgical techniques with extensive clinical experience. His commitment to patient care, innovation, and precision makes him a trusted expert in complex neurological procedures.

Clinical Expertise and Advanced Techniques

  • Awake craniotomy to preserve speech and motor areas during tumor resection.
  • Use of MRI Tractography to map neural pathways before and during surgery.
  • Complex skull base surgeries (transnasal or transcranial) in collaboration with top French centers.
  • Minimally invasive spinal surgery and disc replacement.
  • Tumor resection from areas responsible for facial movement and speech.

Brain Tumor Resection Near Critical Areas

Successfully removed a tumor from the brain region responsible for facial movement and speech, preserving function through precise microsurgical navigation.

Middle Cranial Fossa Meningioma Resection

Performed gross total resection of a large meningioma invading the right transverse sinus. The sinus was opened, tumor removed, and sutured microscopically while preserving the Galen’s vein. Postoperative anticoagulation managed with Lovenox and Rivaroxaban. The patient remains seizure-free and in excellent condition.
